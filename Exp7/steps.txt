### 2. SPSS Procedure (Follow Exactly)

As per your lab manual's "Step 4" and "Step 5," follow these specific instructions to handle the table data:

#### Step A: Weight the Cases

Because we are using a frequency table, you must tell SPSS that the `Count` column represents the number of people.

1. Go to **Data** > **Weight Cases...**.
2. Select **Weight cases by**.
3. Move `Count` into the **Frequency Variable** box.
4. Click **OK**.

#### Step B: Run the Chi-Square Test

1. Go to **Analyze** > **Descriptive Statistics** > **Crosstabs...**.
2. Move `Experience` into the **Row(s)** box.
3. Move `Source` into the **Column(s)** box.
4. Click the **Statistics** button, check the **Chi-square** box, and click **Continue**.
5. Click **OK**.
